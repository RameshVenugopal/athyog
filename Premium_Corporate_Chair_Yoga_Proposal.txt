Executive Overview
In today’s demanding corporate environment, professionals across all industries face long sitting hours, high stress levels, screen fatigue, neck and back discomfort, and reduced energy levels. The Corporate Chair Yoga Wellness Program is a scientifically informed, low-impact, and highly effective solution designed to improve employee health without disrupting productivity.
Program Vision
To create workplaces where employees feel energized, focused, and resilient — where wellness becomes part of the organizational culture.
Why Chair Yoga Works for All Professionals
• No special clothing or equipment required
• Suitable for executives, managers, and staff of all age groups
• Minimal space required
• Low injury risk and adaptable intensity
• Scientifically supported stress and posture benefits
Program Structure
1. Weekly Wellness Sessions (30–45 minutes)
2. Daily Micro-Break Desk Sessions (10–15 minutes)
3. Monthly Awareness & Posture Workshop (60–90 minutes)
Session Components
• Seated spinal mobility exercises
• Neck & shoulder tension release
• Wrist and hand mobility for computer users
• Guided breathing techniques for stress management
• 5-minute relaxation practice

 Performance Benefits:
