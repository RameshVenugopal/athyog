Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2025

Ath Yog Wellness Foundation ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.athyog.in or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you register for classes, workshops, purchase products, or subscribe to newsletters, we collect:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Payment details (collected securely through third-party payment gateways)
  • Date of birth or age information if relevant to specific programs
  • Health-related information or special needs provided voluntarily

b. Usage and Technical Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our site:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, session duration, and click patterns
  • Cookies and other tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your registrations and payments securely
  • Communicate class schedules, updates, and promotional offers
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Your consent (e.g., marketing communications)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., class registration)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., service improvement and security)

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information
  • We share information only with trusted third-party service providers under strict confidentiality
  • We may disclose information if required by law

5. Data Security

We adopt industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site usage. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Opt-out of direct marketing

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@athyog.in.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. International Data Transfer

Your information may be transferred to and processed in India. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Please review this page regularly.
