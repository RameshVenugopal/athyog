Frequently Asked Questions

Is the diet difficult to follow?

No. It is a doctor-approved diet full of nutrition and taste. We focus on nourishment, not starvation, which will get you into the habit of healthy eating.

Can I modify exercises if I have injuries?

Yes! The exercises are actually easy—you won't even believe it—but the impact is mindblowing! We do modifications and gradually one gets fully flexible towards the end of the session.

What if I miss a 5:00 AM session?

Don't worry. Recordings for missed classes are provided, so you can maintain consistency at your own pace whenever needed.

How do I stay motivated throughout?

When the whole crowd is craving and wanting to not stop in 21 days because they have started enjoying—well, we continue for next 21 days on demand! The community keeps you going.
