About the Program

Introduction

Slim Smart Sadhana (SSS) is a structured 21-day yoga-based holistic wellness program designed to help you detoxify, tone, and naturally transform your body. The program integrates yoga therapy, guided pranayama, internal detox practices, mindful movement, and doctor-approved nutrition guidance to improve gut health, enhance lung capacity, boost metabolism, and build lifelong healthy habits—without crash dieting or extreme workouts. By focusing on internal cleansing, conscious eating, and sustainable daily routines, SSS helps you feel lighter, healthier, stronger, and mentally balanced.

About the Coach

Yog Guru Ashwini Patil

Athyog Wellness Foundation is guided by Yog Guru Ashwini Patil, a dedicated yoga practitioner and wellness mentor with over two decades of teaching experience. Her journey in yoga began not just as a discipline, but as a path to healing and transformation. Through her own experience of overcoming physical pain and health challenges, she discovered the profound potential of yoga as a complete mind–body solution. This lived experience deeply shapes her compassionate, practical, and results-oriented teaching style.

Her approach blends traditional yogic wisdom with modern lifestyle understanding, making yoga accessible, effective, and sustainable for today's fast-paced lives.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Slim Smart Sadhana?

SSS is suitable for men and women of all age groups, beginners as well as experienced practitioners. Practices are modified as per individual capacity.

Do I need prior yoga experience?

No. This program is beginner-friendly and taught step by step.

Is this only a weight-loss program?

No. It focuses on overall wellness—gut health, metabolism, energy, mental balance, and lifestyle improvement.

Will there be extreme dieting or starvation?

No. The program follows doctor-approved nutrition guidance without crash dieting or calorie deprivation.

Are detox practices safe?

Yes. Detox practices are introduced only when suitable and under proper guidance.

Can I manage this with my daily routine?

Yes. Early morning sessions and 24x7 recordings offer flexibility.

What results can I expect?

Most participants experience improved digestion, energy, reduced bloating, mental clarity, and body toning. Individual results may vary.
